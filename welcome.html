About Saudi Smart Tourism

Saudi Smart Tourism is a groundbreaking digital initiative designed to showcase the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia's rich cultural heritage, historical landmarks, and modern entertainment destinations to students, residents, and visitors from around the world.

Our platform serves as a comprehensive guide to exploring Saudi Arabia's most iconic destinations, from the ancient heritage sites of Diriyah and AlUla to the vibrant entertainment of Riyadh Season. We aim to make tourism information accessible, engaging, and informative for everyone interested in discovering the Kingdom.

Our Mission

To create awareness and appreciation for Saudi Arabia's diverse tourism offerings while providing practical information that helps visitors plan memorable experiences. We believe that by sharing knowledge about our heritage and culture, we can foster greater understanding and connection between Saudi Arabia and the world.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive Destination Guides: Detailed information about major tourist attractions including historical context, visiting hours, and ticket information
  • Travel Planning Resources: Visa requirements, hotel recommendations, and practical travel tips

  • Interactive Platform: Sign up to access personalized content and participate in tourism surveys

Featured Destinations

Diriyah - The Birthplace of the Kingdom

Diriyah is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the original home of the Saudi royal family. Located on the outskirts of Riyadh, this historic district features stunning mud-brick architecture and tells the story of Saudi Arabia's founding. The restoration project aims to transform Diriyah into one of the world's greatest gathering places for culture and heritage.

AlUla - An Open-Air Museum

AlUla is home to Hegra (Madain Salih), Saudi Arabia's first UNESCO World Heritage Site. This ancient city features well-preserved Nabatean tombs carved into sandstone outcrops. The dramatic landscape of rock formations, canyons, and archaeological wonders makes AlUla one of the Kingdom's most spectacular destinations.

Riyadh Season - Modern Entertainment

Riyadh Season represents Saudi Arabia's vibrant present and future. This annual entertainment festival features world-class concerts, sporting events, exhibitions, and dining experiences. It showcases the Kingdom's commitment to becoming a global hub for culture, entertainment, and innovation.
